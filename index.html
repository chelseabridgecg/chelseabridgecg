Who are we?

We are a connect group associated with Holy Trinity Brompton. We attend the 18:30 service at HTB Onslow Square and we meet on Tuesday evenings at 19:00 at Church Home. We are a group of social young professionals who value deep community rooted in Jesus Christ along with a good time.

Weekend Away Reflections 2022

Making a home

  • The early church “had everything in common” and communal. What does that look like in our community and our context?
  • Do we really want to look like Jesus?? Are we up for this challenge?
  • We want to create a home for the homeless, a place for the lost.
  • How do we bring in the people from the fringes and bring them into the centre?
  • Creating opportunities for people to spend more time together.

Emphasis on confession and closeness

  • Do we really want to come further in. What does confession look like – openness, vulnerability and support? There’s a lot of fear around this.
  • Be willing to share more of yourself in order to see others for who they are.
  • Talk about things when you're in the middle of it, not just after the effect. We should actively listen - Do you feel listened to, do you feel understood, do you feel loved?
  • Need more vulnerability in our culture on a Tuesday. Permission to respond sharply. With vulnerability comes accountability. Ask "would you like me to hold you accountable to that?"

Setting up and taking a stake in CG

  • Showing up. Being present in people's lives.
  • We should turn up even when we’re tired. We ought to challenge the myth that community doesn't equal rest. The community of God is what we NEED
  • Sharing just isn't about halving problems. Sharing your joy brings more joy to everyone
  • Be vulnerable with people who haven't earned it yet means being vulnerable on Tuesdays

Practical Ideas

  • Support genuine practical needs. Can I borrow money, can someone pick up groceries for me, I'm going out for a walk do you wanna come? Being physically present in life.
  • Can we practically find out who's close to us geographically.
  • Can call and chat to people when walking.
  • Need to be open with our calendars and schedules.
  • Twice a quarter we have a dedicated event. E.g. quarterly and termly events like Shabbat meals. We’ll create an events calendar for this.
  • Celebrations when things come together
  • Fasting as a community, soaking and seeking the Lord in it

Tuesday Specific

  • Let's start at 7 sharp. Worship at 7:10. Session at 7:30. Doing so is respectful of people's time.
  • Aaron needs to cull the WhatsApp group. We’ll also go back to people attending for 3 weeks and committing before adding to the group to stop it getting unwieldly.
  • Say on the WhatsApp group when new people are joining so we know in advance and can welcome them.
