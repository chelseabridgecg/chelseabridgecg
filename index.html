Welcome Home

Who are we?

We are a connect group associated with Holy Trinity Brompton. We attend the 18:30 service at HTB Onslow Square and we meet on Tuesday evenings at 19:00 at Church Home. We are a group of social young professionals who value deep community rooted in Jesus Christ along with a good time.

Announcements.

Group is up and running as normal!

We are currently running a weekly study in the gospel of Luke. Come along and join us for some intentional time of community and learning. You can also join us at the pub on Sunday evening after the 18:30 service at HTB Onslow Square. Please see contact information for how to get in touch.
